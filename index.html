Origin of Pasta

Pasta has a long and debated history, with many scholars suggesting it originated in China over 4000 years ago. Some credit the ancient Romans with making early versions of pasta. It is widely believed that Marco Polo introduced pasta to Italy after his travels to China, but this claim has been challenged.

Spread Across the World

As pasta-making techniques evolved in Italy, the food spread to other parts of Europe, especially during the Renaissance. Italians refined pasta recipes, pairing it with various sauces like tomato-based marinara.
